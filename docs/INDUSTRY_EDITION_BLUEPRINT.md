# Industry Edition Blueprint

Date: 2026-06-04

## Edition Strategy

Industry editions should be preconfigured bundles: modules, fields, workflows, dashboards, reports, templates, AI prompts, and onboarding checklists. They should not be separate products unless regulation or data model depth requires it.

| Edition | Additional Features | Additional Reports | Additional Workflows |
|---|---|---|---|
| School ERP | Admissions CRM, student/parent records, fee reminders, timetable, transport, staff HRMS | Admission funnel, fee aging, attendance, staff cost | Inquiry to admission, fee follow-up, parent communication |
| College ERP | Admissions campaigns, placement CRM, accreditation PMS, faculty workload, committee management | Placement dashboard, accreditation evidence, faculty utilization | Admission to enrollment, accreditation project, placement drive |
| Hospital ERP | Patient inquiry CRM, appointment/referral flow, staff roster, asset/helpdesk, consent ledger | Inquiry conversion, doctor utilization, service SLA, compliance | Inquiry to appointment, discharge follow-up, equipment issue |
| Manufacturing ERP | Distributor CRM, quote/order tracking, shift workforce, quality issue workflow, production project templates | Dealer sales, production task variance, labor cost, quality defects | Dealer quote to order, quality issue to corrective action |
| Construction ERP | WBS/BOQ, subcontractor/vendor costs, site diary, progress photos, critical path, payment milestones | Cost variance, site progress, subcontractor performance, billing milestones | Tender to project, site progress approval, client billing |
| Audit Firm ERP | Engagement templates, evidence vault, review notes, partner approvals, client portal | Engagement status, pending evidence, utilization, review backlog | Client onboarding, audit planning, evidence review, signoff |
| CA Firm ERP | Compliance calendar, recurring client jobs, document checklist, tax/audit/GST task packs | Filing deadline, client pending docs, staff workload, billing | Monthly compliance, document collection, filing approval |
| Tax Practice ERP | Notice management, tax filing tasks, client reminders, document intake | Deadline calendar, notice status, collection, client profitability | Notice intake to response, filing prep, payment reminder |
| IT Company ERP | Lead-to-project, agile delivery, sprint/release, Git integration, resource utilization | Sprint health, project margin, utilization, pipeline-to-delivery | Deal won to project, sprint execution, timesheet to invoice |
