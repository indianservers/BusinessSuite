# Executive Dashboard Blueprint

Date: 2026-06-04

## CEO Dashboard

| Widgets | KPIs | Charts | Alerts |
|---|---|---|---|
| Business command center, AI daily brief, revenue/delivery/people/cash cards | ARR/MRR, sales pipeline, project margin, attrition, cash risk | Revenue trend, customer health, operating score | Red projects, payroll blockers, high-value deal risk, collection risk |

| Widgets | KPIs | Charts | Alerts |
|---|---|---|---|
| Delivery health, capacity, SLA, operational exceptions | On-time delivery, utilization, overdue work, SLA breaches | Portfolio heatmap, resource load, cycle time | Capacity conflict, blocked milestone, workflow backlog |
